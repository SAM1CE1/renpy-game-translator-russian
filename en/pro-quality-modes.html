Practical benefits

Adjust per game

Different visual novels need different balances between pace and translation quality.

Less locked into one workflow

You do not have to use the exact same behavior for a short demo and a long story-heavy VN.

Easier to compare results

You can choose the mode that better matches what you want from a specific project.

Other PRO capabilities

AI translation with scene context
Makes scenes easier to read with the right tone, not just the right basic meaning.
Fallback service
Gives you a calmer workflow if the main translation path is temporarily unavailable.
Custom dictionary
Names and terms drift less from scene to scene.
