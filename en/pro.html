Why PRO

What PRO solves in real play sessions

The Base version is a good entry point. PRO matters when you want not just translation, but a more controlled and predictable result.

1

Context makes lines read better

Nearby lines and scene tone matter in places where literal translation weakens the meaning.

2

Interface clarity also matters

In a long game, it helps to see more than dialogue in Russian: menus, buttons, history, and utility screens also matter.

3

Long sessions do not like fragile workflows

If the main path becomes unavailable, PRO is better prepared to keep going without breaking the flow.

4

A weak line should be fixable immediately

With PRO, you can correct a problem line during the scene instead of waiting for a later update or editing everything outside the game.

5

One configuration does not fit every game

Different VNs need different dictionaries and tone. Profiles help keep those setups separate.

6

Names and terms should stay stable

The dictionary in PRO helps lock your preferred wording so it changes less from scene to scene.
