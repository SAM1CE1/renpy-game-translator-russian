In-game translation editing

Useful when you do not want to tolerate a bad line until the end of the current scene.

How it worksBack to PRO

How it works

1

You notice a weak line

During gameplay, it quickly becomes obvious when a specific line needs better wording.

2

You edit inside the game

Instead of switching to external files, you stay in the current scene and adjust the line in place.

3

You save the preferred version

The corrected wording is kept for later so the same problem does not keep coming back.

4

You keep reading without breaking flow

That matters most in long scenes where leaving the game would ruin the pacing.

Practical benefits

Immediate fixes

You do not need to postpone corrections or rebuild anything outside the game.

Easier to keep your own wording

If you already know how the line should sound, PRO gives you a direct way to preserve it.

Best for important scenes

The more emotional or important the moment is, the more valuable immediate correction becomes.

Other PRO capabilities

Context-aware AI translation
Keeps nearby lines and scene tone in view for a more natural result.
Custom dictionary
Lock names and important terms.
Game profiles
Keep settings and dictionaries separate between different titles.
